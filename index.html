Seed History

Neutral Settlements (marked with "N" on the board):

  1. Place neutral settlements at the positions marked with gray "N" circles on the board vertices
  2. Each player starts with:
    • 2 settlements and 2 roads (place after neutral settlements)
    • Standard starting resources from second settlement
  3. Gameplay: Neutral settlements block building positions but don't collect resources
  4. Victory Points: First to 10 points wins (standard rules apply)

💡 Why neutral settlements?

They balance the game by limiting expansion and preventing one player from dominating resource-rich areas, creating strategic tension similar to 3-4 player games.
